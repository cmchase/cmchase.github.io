Oh, hey

I'm Cory Chase and I'm the lead web developer for Loop Management in Clearwater, FL. I handle the administrative front-end stuff for a large web application and generally keep software engineers from making terrible, terrible design decisions.

I've been professionally active in the industry since early 2003 but, like many, I got my start in the late 90's with things better left forgotten on Geocities and Angelfire.

I tend to focus front-end design. I've got tons of experience with Photoshop, HTML, PHP, CSS and jQuery, but these days I find myself looking at C# and ASP.NET more than I'd have ever thought. At the end of the day, interfaces and user experience are what float my boat.

To be honest, this blog is meant to help me flex my writing muscles and I don't expect others to get a lot out of what I have to say, but I can be contacted at cmchase@gmail.com.
